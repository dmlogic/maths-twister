Welcome to Maths Twister

How to play

  • First, read all the instructions
  • When you are ready, spin the wheel
  • The wheel will tell you where to move your body, which an adult will read out
  • When you are in place, your adult will tell you a calculation (which will be on the screen) and you will have seconds to answer
  • The start timer button will start your countdown
  • Shout out your answer before the timer ends
  • After your seconds is up, the answer will appear on the screen
  • You can repeat until all players have fallen
